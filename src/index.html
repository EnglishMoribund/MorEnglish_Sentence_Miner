MORENGLISH SENTENCE MINER

◆

v0.3.4 — mine English sentences, tag their grammar, forge diagrams.

A retro FF7-styled desktop app for English grammar analysis. Paste or clip a sentence, split it into segments, tag words and phrases from a searchable grammar registry, then export diagrams, Markdown, HTML ruby, or study decks.

Optional AI plugins (Ollama, Anthropic, or any CLI), a local sentence library, custom tags, and a loopback connector API / MCP server for scripts and agents. AI suggestions are always reviewed before they apply.
